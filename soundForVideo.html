Sound Art Fall 2016

Project 2 -- MIDTERM -- Crit: 10/13 + 10/18

For your midterm project you will create the sound for a video clip. This video clip can be from a project that you did yourself, it can be the film of a friend, or it can be anything you find on the internetz or elsewhere. It should be 2-5 minutes in length. When choosing your clip please keep in mind that the clip you choose should be well designed for sound, as Randy Thom says. It should offer you opportunitie to create expressive, dynamic sound. It should also give you a chance to create multiple different sonic environments. Your finished midterm project should include the following different types of film sound: ambient recordings, voice recordings, hard fx and music. 30 percent of your midterm grade is dependent upon a review of your rough draft of your midterm project by 10/ 11. Good luck!
